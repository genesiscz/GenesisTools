# ADHD diverge — Infinite-budget (10yr maximalist) frame

Vantage: infinite compute, infinite engineers, a decade — what is the category-defining DevDashboard that becomes the default nervous-system for every developer and AI-agent operator on earth? Moonshots, platform plays, hardware, ambient/spatial, autonomous agents, on-device AI.

1. **Spatial agent-operations room you wear, not hold** — a Vision-Pro/AR-glasses layer where each agent session, container, and metric is a physical object you arrange around your desk, glance-steer, and hand off to a teammate by pointing. `[feature]`
2. **On-device fleet brain that predicts and pre-empts** — a phone-resident model trained on your machines' history that says "this agent is about to loop / this disk fills in 40 min / this build will OOM" and acts before you look. `[feature]`
3. **The cryptographic trust standard everyone else licenses** — open-source the verifiable E2E transport, get it audited into a named protocol ("DevDash Attested Channel"), then license the certification + SDK to every other remote-dev/IDE/CI vendor. `[moat]`
4. **Operators-of-record for autonomous agents** — sell to ops/SRE and eventually insurers/auditors: a tamper-evident black-box flight recorder of every agent action with cryptographic provenance, so an agent's work is admissible and insurable. `[audience]`
5. **Voice-first ambient co-pilot in the car, kitchen, on a walk** — full hands-free narration + steering of your dev machine through an earpiece, so "monitoring your build" becomes a thing you do while living your life. `[feature]`
6. **Agent-compute marketplace metered through the dashboard** — your idle dev machines (and a managed cloud pool) become rentable agent runners; the dashboard is the broker and takes a cut of every agent-hour traded. `[monetization]`
7. **Bio-adaptive operator wellbeing layer** — pull HR/HRV/sleep from the wearable already on the user's wrist and gate dangerous actions (force-push to prod, agent auto-merge) when the operator is fatigued or stressed, becoming the safety conscience of solo builders. `[feature]`
8. **Education + certification flywheel: "Agent Operations" as a discipline** — a maximalist academy + cert that teaches a whole generation to supervise AI agents from the dashboard, making the app the textbook and the credential the hiring signal — owning the talent pipeline. `[moat]`
