No-see via E2E

"Your machine. Your keys. We can't see your data — and you can prove it."

Pairing keys are generated on-device and never escrowed. The protocol is open and the clients are auditable, so you don't have to trust the marketing — you can read the code, inspect the handshake, and confirm the relay only ever carries ciphertext.

  • Keys in your phone's secure enclave + your Mac only

Pair in ~30 seconds

Three steps. No cloud account required.

01

Run the agent on your Mac

One command starts the DevDashboard Agent. It serves your terminals and Pulse locally — nothing leaves the machine yet.

$ devdash agent start
02

Pick your transport

LAN, Tailscale, your own Cloudflare tunnel, or managed. The wizard sets up the path you choose and hands you a pairing QR.

LAN TS CF Mgd
03

Scan & verify on your phone

Scan the QR, compare safety numbers, and you're live. Keys stay on your two devices — confirm the channel is end-to-end and you're done.
