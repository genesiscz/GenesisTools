Trust, not promises

Pick exactly how far
your bytes travel.

One switchable transport layer, four trust tiers. The further right you go, the more convenient — and we designed every tier so the vendor still can’t read your terminal. You don’t take our word for it; you verify it.

Zero third party

LAN

Same Wi-Fi, phone talks straight to your Mac. Nothing leaves your network — no relay, no tunnel, no account. The simplest possible path.

Verify: it literally has nowhere else to go

Trust-max

Tailscale / WireGuard

A private, end-to-end encrypted mesh between your devices. Reach your machine from anywhere with nobody in the middle — the traffic is yours alone, edge to edge.

Verify: standard WireGuard keys, on your own tailnet

Your own account

Self-hosted Cloudflare tunnel

A one-command guided wizard stands up a tunnel under your Cloudflare account. Public reach without port-forwarding — and we, the vendor, are never in the path.

Verify: it’s your domain, your tunnel, your dashboard

One-tap · E2E

Managed relay

The easiest path: one tap, remote access anywhere. We relay the connection — but the payload is sealed by app-layer end-to-end encryption. The keys live only on your phone and your Mac. We carry the envelope; we can’t open it.

Verify: pin the key fingerprint & audit the open client

The promise we can prove
