Trust, verifiable

Four ways in.
One promise you can check yourself.

Pick a transport on a single switchable layer. On three of them, no third party — not even us — is ever in the path. On the fourth, we relay only ciphertext, with the keys living solely on your devices.

Self-hosted tunnel

A guided wizard stands up cloudflared on your own Cloudflare account. One command, QR-paired. The vendor is never in your data path.

We can't see your data. Your account, your tunnel.

No-see via E2E

Managed relay

One-tap remote. We relay only encrypted bytes — an X25519 handshake puts the keys on your phone and Mac alone. We can't read your contents.

Honest caveat: as a relay we can see connection metadata (timing, volume) — never the contents.

The proof

Don't take our word. Verify it.

A trust claim you can't check is just marketing. So we made every layer of it inspectable — with your own eyes, on your own devices.

  • Compare the fingerprint

    The pairing key shows on phone and Mac. Same six bytes = no one in the middle.

  • Open-source client

    The app and agent are auditable. Read the crypto, not a datasheet about it.

  • Reproducible builds

    Rebuild from source, match the published hash. What ships is what's on GitHub.

  • Ciphertext on the wire

    Capture the managed relay traffic yourself — it's opaque AEAD frames, end to end.

Pair in ~30 seconds

Three steps. No account required.

  1. 01

    Run the agent

    Scan with your phone
